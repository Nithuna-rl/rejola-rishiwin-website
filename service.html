Our Services

Bill of Quantities (BoQ):

The Bill of Quantities (BoQ) serves as a detailed list of materials, labour, and costs required for a construction project. It provides clarity and transparency by outlining the quantities and specifications of each item needed, enabling accurate cost estimation and budgeting. We specialize in offering BoQ as a service that ensures meticulous documentation, facilitating smooth procurement and project management processes.

Estimation

Estimation is the cornerstone of any successful construction project, providing an accurate assessment of costs and the resources required. We use advanced processes and techniques clubbed with industry expertise to forecast project expenses with precision, helping our clients make informed choices and decisions and thereby optimize budget allocation. With meticulous attention to detail, we ensure that our estimates align with project objectives & expectations.

Estimation for Central and State Government Projects

Estimation service that meets the unique requirements of Central and State Government projects, adhering to regulatory standards and procurement guidelines. With our extensive experience in public sector contracts, we provide comprehensive cost assessments that comply with government regulations and provide transparency in project financing and funding as well. Our expertise ensures that government projects are accurately budgeted and effectively managed.

Bar Bending Schedule (BBS)

The Bar Bending Schedule (BBS) is an important document that details the specifications and quantities of reinforcement steel bars required for construction projects. Our BBS service offers meticulous planning and scheduling of reinforcement works, ensuring structural integrity and compliance with the required engineering standards. By accurately quantifying and arranging reinforcement bars from trusted brands, we optimize construction efficiency and minimize material wastage, contributing to the overall success of the project.

Project Management and Supervision

Our project management service ensures the overseeing and the execution of the construction project from start to finish. This includes coordinating with subcontractors, managing timelines, ensuring quality control, and handling any unforeseen challenges or circumstances that may arise during when the construction is in progress.

Quality Assurance and Quality Control (QA/QC)

We provide QC Audit and certification as well. We implement a complete QA/QC program to ensure that construction materials, workmanship, and processes meet the required standards and specifications. This involves conducting regular inspections, testing materials for compliance, and addressing any non-conformities promptly to maintain the project's quality standards.

Health, Safety, and Environmental (HSE) Management

Provide HSE management services to prioritize the health and safety of workers on-site and mitigate potential environmental impacts associated with construction activities. This includes developing and implementing safety protocols, conducting safety training programs, and ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements to create a safe working environment.

Documentation and Reporting

We offer comprehensive documentation and reporting services to maintain accurate records of project activities, progress, expenses, and other much-needed/ crucial aspects. This involves preparing regular progress reports for clients and stakeholders concerned, documenting any changes or variations to the original scope of work or design, and maintaining detailed records of project documentation such as contracts, permits, and approvals.

Tender Working

Tender working involves the preparation and submission of competitive bids for construction projects. Our Tender Working service combines strategic planning, thorough documentation, and industry understanding to craft bids that stand out in the competition. With a focus on accuracy and compliance, we assist clients in navigating the tendering process smoothly, maximizing their chances of securing contracts and joint venture partnerships.

Material Schedule

A Material Schedule outlines the procurement and delivery timelines for construction materials required throughout the project from inception to closure. Our Material Schedule service ensures efficient inventory management and timely supply of materials, preventing delays and minimizing risks also any unforeseen issues. By coordinating closely with suppliers, we enhance material usage and keep in control procurement costs, enhancing project profitability and performance.

Client Billing

Billing is normally seen as a tedious process by many firms and concerns. Client Billing involves the accurate calculation and invoicing of project costs to clients based on completed milestones or work stages. Our Client Billing service streamlines the invoicing process, providing transparent and detailed billing statements that reflect project progress and expenditures. With a focus on accuracy and accountability, we ensure timely payment collection and foster positive client relationships, facilitating project success and client satisfaction.
